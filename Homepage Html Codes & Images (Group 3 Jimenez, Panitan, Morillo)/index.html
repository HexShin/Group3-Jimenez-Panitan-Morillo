What Makes Our Coffee Shop Special?

Kopi Kount is a local coffee shop situated in the bustling Barangay East Rembo, Taguig City. Established amidst the challenges of the pandemic in 2021. This small business has been serving the community for approximately three years, offering a unique coffee experience that resonates with its patrons. With its inviting ambiance, delectable coffee brews, and warm hospitality, Kopi Kount has established itself as a cherished gathering spot, fostering connections and spreading joy one cup at a time.
